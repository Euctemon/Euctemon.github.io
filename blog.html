I never though I would be interested in logic in slightest. Do you need logic for ordinary life? Do you get in touch with logic at all? The interesting thing is that the word logical is used by people quite often. Some people might say they are logical thinkers, possibly as opposed to erratic or chaotic. People might say that something is illogical. If you look at synonyms, you might find words like inconsistent, absurd, false, implausible, unreasonable or even unwise. In a book Being logical the author says that logical people are those who are attentive, get facts straight, ensure their ideas are clear, are mindful of the origins of ideas, match ideas to facts and words to ideas. They also communicate effectively and avoid vague or ambiguous language.
