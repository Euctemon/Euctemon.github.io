Hi, my name is David and these are my personal websites where I share the things I am interested in. I enjoy clear and beautifully presented explanations, and I try to do the same when writing about a topic I like.

I am interested in application of logic and algebra, so for example theorem proving, program verification and SAT solving. As a graduated mechanical engineer, I am also interested in physics, specifically in continuum mechanics and things connected to it like numerical programming and functional analysis.
